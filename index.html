Bakos János

Bakos Gallery have been specialising in Hungarian art and Old Master paintings for over 35 years. Our gallery, located on Budapest's historic Heroes Square, is one of the largest commercial gallery spaces in Buda and showcases 500 years of Hungarian art and portrait miniatures from the 1800-1900 period through to the late 20th century.
